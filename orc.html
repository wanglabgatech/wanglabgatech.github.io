Physics Field Day

In April 2024, Prof. Wang participated in the Physics Field Day Activities organized by the Society of Physics Students (SPS) at Georgia Tech and showed high school students the cutting-edge research equipment in his lab.

AAUW Tech Trek

In June and July 2024, Prof. Wang participate in the GIFT program organized by Georgia Tech Center for Education Integrating Science, Mathematics, and Computing (CEISMC) to provide internship for high school teacher to work in his research lab and develop innovative curricula for use in the classroom.
